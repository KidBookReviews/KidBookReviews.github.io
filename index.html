I am back here and today I will be writing a review on "Crickets Countryside" George Selden. This is a AMAZING follow up to "The Cricket in Times Square" and a great book in general! One of the BEST books I have ever seen plopped up before my eyes! Before you read the review on this book I would recommend you either read the first book, or look at its review. This book is about a cricket (from the previous book) who's meadow is being destroyed by tractors. While Tucker the mouse and Harry cat get plucked from the city life to the country life, to help the cricket save his meadow. Will Tucker be able to do anything about it? Or will the meadow go up in smithereens? Read the book to find out! I have to say this is a great book, which is probably even better than the first one! Which is why I give this book.. 5 STARS!
